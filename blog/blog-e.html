Jyakucyou and war

2025-05-28T14:30 2025-06 2025-07 2025-08
This is a brochure from an exhibition held at the Tokushima Prefectural Museum of Literature and Calligraphy.I was too far away to go, so I was sent the pamphlet just to have it.


NHK's morning drama 'Anpan' shows just what it is like at this time of year.The pamphlet also mentioned that Jakucho Setouchi believed in the Imperial Rescript on Education, like Nobu, a character in the drama.


I remember when I was in Joboji, my daughter used to watch Anpanman books and videos in the library.For many writers of this generation, the experience of war is at the core of their expression.
